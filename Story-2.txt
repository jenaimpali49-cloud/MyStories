                                  ""The Hidden Truth""
                                  =====================


Ilaara led him through streets where the air hummed with ancient power.

“This kingdom exists between moments,” she explained. “We guard the balance between the human world and the unseen 
forces that shape it.”

They stopped before a vast tree whose roots floated in the air like drifting clouds.

“The Root of Tides is dying,” she said. “When it fails, your world will lose its rains. Rivers will fade. Fields will 
turn to dust.”

Kavin’s chest tightened. His village depended on the monsoon. Without rain, they would not survive.

“Why me?” he asked.

“Because your heart carries something rare,” Ilaara replied. “You still believe the world can be saved.”
